# Chronicle Status

## Current Focus

Morning pages, journaling, writing, mindfulness, meditation, and the practice
of preserving continuity of attention and voice. Current center is a small,
busy-proof routine that captures useful material without turning the wiki into
a diary.

## Recent Changes

- Chronicle was synthesized into a project hub with pages for morning pages and
  life OS, travel/environment design, writing practice, and meditation.
- Overlap with Whetstone was made explicit.

## Decisions

- Keep raw journal texture out of the wiki unless it becomes a durable method,
  claim, or project decision.
- Use Chronicle for lived practice and Whetstone for reusable cognitive
  technique.
- Treat weekly notes as deltas and promotion queues rather than copied project
  dashboards.

## Next Actions

- Define the smallest daily Chronicle routine that survives busy weeks.
- Add a rolling weekly note only if it reduces copy-paste and improves project
  review.
- Use a "Promote To Weaver" section in weekly review for material that should
  become durable wiki state.

## Open Questions

- What belongs in a repo-native weekly note versus the main Obsidian vault?
- How should raw pages be harvested without creating a diary archive?
- Which weekly review sections are useful enough to keep?
- How much state vocabulary helps before it becomes self-monitoring noise?

## Blocked Or Waiting

- Waiting on workflow decision for weekly notes and project dashboard assembly.

## Promote To Wiki

- Stable weekly review format.
- Durable journaling or meditation methods.
- Writing exercises or voice practices that become repeatable.
- State-design observations that generalize beyond one week.
