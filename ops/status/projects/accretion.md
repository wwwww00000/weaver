# Accretion Status

## Current Focus

Personal finance and wealth accumulation. Current center is policy: balance
sheet tracking, liquidity and safe allocation, structured-product evaluation,
bounded option-selling study, and finance tooling that supports decisions
without encouraging extra risk.

## Recent Changes

- Accretion was synthesized into a project hub with pages for balance policy,
  structured products, option selling, and finance tooling/data.
- Residual finance notes were routed into existing Accretion pages.

## Decisions

- Separate durable finance policy from dated account or card status.
- Treat structured products, options, and finance-engineering ideas as bounded
  study unless policy explicitly permits risk.
- Route finance software build plans to Genesis when they become implementation
  projects.
