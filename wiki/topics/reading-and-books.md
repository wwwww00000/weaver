# Reading Notes And Books

The book queue is a cross-project shelf. It should not become a library
catalog. Its job is to preserve reading that changes practices, frames, or
project decisions.

The useful pattern is:

```text
book -> claim -> practice or lens -> destination page
```

If a book does not change a practice, question, or project frame, it can stay
as a source artifact.

## Books As Practice Inputs

Several books are already absorbed into active project pages:

- `The Artist's Way` supports Chronicle morning pages, creative recovery, and
  Conjuration artist-date style replenishment.
- `Waking Up` supports Chronicle meditation and nondual inquiry.
- `Willpower Doesn't Work` supports Chronicle environment design and
  peak-experience capture.
- `The Extended Mind` supports Whetstone and Genesis views of cognition as
  embodied, environmental, and social.

The common move is to translate book advice into repeatable practices:

- write morning pages before organizing;
- capture state-dependent insights with a small re-entry handle;
- use movement, sketching, and conversation as thinking tools;
- design environments that make reflection or action easier;
- schedule artist dates, play, or novelty as inputs rather than rewards.

## Productivity And Life Systems

The GTD, Bullet Journal, Kanban, Pomodoro, ZTD, Autofocus, and Eisenhower
material resolves into a simple operating stack:

- capture loose commitments and ideas outside working memory;
- clarify the next action;
- organize by project, context, waiting, someday, and reference;
- visualize work in progress when sequencing matters;
- choose the next task by importance, context, energy, and time;
- review regularly enough that the system remains trusted.

This belongs in Chronicle only when it supports reflection and continuity. It
belongs in Genesis only when it becomes tooling. It belongs in Whetstone when
the interesting object is attention, focus, or skill acquisition.

## Wholeheartedness, Play, And Creative Recovery

Brene Brown's material and the play notes point at a humane counterweight to
pure agency framing. Useful recurring themes:

- worthiness and self-compassion reduce perfectionism as a hidden blocker;
- vulnerability is a connection and courage practice, not only a social risk;
- play and rest are not status failures;
- creativity should be protected from comparison and constant productivity
  pressure;
- play history can reveal what kinds of exploration, competition, collecting,
  storytelling, movement, or directing actually replenish energy.

Chronicle should own the practice layer. Conjuration can borrow the play and
artist-date material when it directly feeds imaginative output.

## Mastery And Flow

The Kotler, Waitzkin, and learning-mastery sources converge on engineered
practice rather than passive inspiration.

Useful rules:

- choose a challenge just beyond current ability;
- make feedback fast and concrete;
- decompose complex skills into bottleneck drills;
- use constraints to create creative pressure;
- reflect on failures and update the practice design;
- track flow inputs such as clarity, novelty, challenge, feedback, and stakes.

This material belongs mostly in Whetstone. Genesis can use it when designing
build sprints, and Conjuration can use it when designing drawing or imagination
practice.

## Work, Business, And Economic Frames

The business and economics books are lenses for project and finance decisions:

- `Company of One`: growth is a strategy, not a default goal; optimize for
  profitability, autonomy, simplicity, and enough.
- `So Good They Can't Ignore You`: rare and valuable traits often come from
  building career capital through excellent work, then cashing it in for
  autonomy, impact, or challenge.
- `The Infinite Game`: long-running projects should optimize for durability,
  values, adaptability, and worthy rivals rather than short-term wins.
- Georgism and Piketty: land, capital, inheritance, and AI-era returns are
  structural lenses for wealth, policy, and Accretion context.

These sources do not yet require separate finance or economics pages. They are
available as context for Accretion, Genesis, and future life-policy review.

## Fiction And Imagination Reading

The reading note frames fiction as imagination exercise. For Conjuration and
Chronicle, fiction reading can be active:

- visualize scenes, blocking, camera angles, and character bodies;
- read manga or illustrated material for panel composition and shape language;
- sample literary magazines to calibrate taste and voice;
- extract one motif, scene device, or phrase form into a prompt.

The goal is not to read everything. The goal is to use reading as input for
imagination, writing, drawing, and taste.
