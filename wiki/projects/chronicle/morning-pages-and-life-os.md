# Morning Pages And Life OS

Morning pages are useful because they create a bounded, permissive attention
container. The paper is not the essential part. The essential loop is:
commit to a short session, lower distraction, let material appear without
premature editing, keep a rhythm anchor, and preserve enough of the result for
later retrieval.

Chronicle should treat pages as a capture and state-regulation practice, not
only as writing output.

## Page Variants

Use different variants for different constraints.

- Audio pages: speak continuously for 8 to 12 minutes, then add a tiny index of
  keywords or themes.
- One-note scroll: keep a single running note and write without branching into
  organization.
- Head pages: think through a page while walking or commuting, then capture a
  handle, thesis, and next action afterward.
- Prompted pages: start from one narrow prompt when the blank page is too much.

The retrieval step matters. Without it, pages relieve pressure but do not
compound. A weekly pass should skim or replay recent captures and extract
seeds, themes, repeated loops, and next experiments.

## Life OS Loop

The life operating system material can be simplified into four verbs:
capture, clarify, commit, and care.

Capture collects the loose material: ideas, worries, urges, observations,
creative fragments, open loops. Clarify turns the material into a small number
of meanings or options. Commit chooses a next action, experiment, or posture.
Care keeps the system humane by checking sleep, food, movement, solitude,
connection, and recovery.

This is not meant to become an elaborate productivity system. The useful
minimum is a daily boot, a midday checkpoint, a shutdown pass, and a weekly
review.

## State Routing

Think tags can serve as a state router for Chronicle.

- State tags: calm, scattered, overclocked, low, irritable, tender.
- Mode tags: execute, explore, connect, recover, play, open awareness.
- Risk tags: doomscroll, avoidance, perfection loop, urgency trance.
- Need tags: sleep, food, movement, connection, solitude.

The point is not a complete taxonomy. The point is to notice enough structure
to choose the next right-sized move.

## Attention Hygiene

The "dopamine detox" material is best interpreted as stimulus control rather
than a literal detox. Chronicle can use it as background hygiene: reduce
high-cue loops, remove push triggers, add friction around feeds and news, park
the phone, and replace compulsive checks with reading, walking, journaling,
exercise, or deep work.

The important test is whether boredom becomes tolerable enough for writing,
meditation, and problem solving to resume.
